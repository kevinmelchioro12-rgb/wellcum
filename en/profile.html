Big Five · OCEAN

Your psychometric profile

Five dimensions capture much of what makes each of us unique. Answer 50 statements honestly and discover where you stand on the Big Five traits — openness, conscientiousness, extraversion, agreeableness and neuroticism (emotional stability).

The questionnaire draws on the public-domain markers of the International Personality Item Pool (IPIP), the same framework behind instruments such as the IPIP-NEO and the BFI-2. It is not a clinical diagnosis — it is a mirror to know yourself a little better.

This tool is for information and self-knowledge only and does not replace a professional psychological assessment. Scores reflect your position relative to the response scale, not a population-normed percentile. Your answers stay on your device and are never sent to any server.

Sources: Goldberg, L. R. (1992) · International Personality Item Pool · Soto & John (2017), BFI-2 · Costa & McCrae, NEO-PI-R.
