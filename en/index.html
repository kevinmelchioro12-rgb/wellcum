01 — Club & Philosophy

Wellcum, a unique sensory experience

Wellcum is Austria's largest and most renowned FKK club: across 7,000 m² it brings together under one roof a world of FKK experiences, spa, bar/restaurant and hotel. Located just 6 km from the Italian border, it attracts an international audience — particularly from Italy, Slovenia and Austria.

The modern facility with 43 hotel rooms offers maximum privacy, supported by a dedicated team of more than 60 staff members who communicate in several languages (DE, IT, EN, SL, RU, UA, ES).

02 — Spa & Wellness

360° Wellness — our Spa for men

The Wellcum Spa is the ideal place for a relaxing break: saunas, heated pool, relaxation areas and beneficial massages. On arrival you will receive sterilised slippers, a bathrobe and, on request, a safe for your valuables.

In the pool, surrounded by fantastic girls

Outdoor pool heated to 30 °C all year round — outdoors in summer, among the snow-covered pines in winter. The outdoor area of over 2,800 m² invites you to sunbathe and switch off, with a Finnish sauna, terrace and lawn.

First relaxation, then fun

Five saunas await you — including two classic Finnish ones at around 90 °C: one in the outdoor area, the other in the exclusive VIP lounge. They boost circulation and detoxify the skin. For the perfect refresh: a dip in the pool!

Put yourself in the hands of our masseuses

Authentic massages performed by professionals, every day from 2:00 pm to 10:00 pm.

Your palate will be spoiled too

The bar and restaurant area is the meeting point of the Wellcum — ideal for socialising with guests and girls, even during meals. Included in admission: two alcoholic drinks (Spritz, beer, Radler) and unlimited soft drinks from the dispensers. Fine wines, cocktails and champagne à la carte.

At lunch a hot and cold buffet; in the evening, show cooking right before your eyes. In summer, barbecue evenings on Thursdays and Sundays from 6:00 pm. For groups of at least six people we prepare, on request, exclusive specialities such as scampi or lobster — always fresh from Carinthia.

07 — Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know

How much does admission to the Wellcum cost?

The admission price is 90 euros.

What is included in the admission price?

Use of the SPA and the pool, two drinks of your choice (beer, spritz or radler), unlimited soft drinks from the dispensers and an all-you-can-eat buffet during the restaurant's opening hours.

Can I stay overnight at the club?

The club adjoins the partner hotel, which offers 43 rooms. Visit the hotel's website →

Do I need to bring a bathrobe and pool slippers?

A bathrobe and slippers are provided on arrival. You may still bring your own if you wish.

What should I wear under the bathrobe?

You can decide for yourself: nothing or a swimsuit. To access the restaurant, wearing the bathrobe is mandatory.

Is the outdoor pool open in winter too?

Yes, the pool is open all year round. In winter the water is heated to 30 °C.

Can I reach the Wellcum by train?

Yes. The nearest stations are Arnoldstein and Villach, connected to the main stations of Northern Italy.

Is there a taxi service from the station?

Several 24-hour taxi services are available.

What is the nearest Italian airport?

Venice airport, about two and a half hours by car.

What is the nearest Austrian airport?

Klagenfurt airport.

How do you get to the club by car?

The fastest route is the motorway from Udine, the A23, which becomes the A2 in Austria. Exits: Tarvisio or Arnoldstein.

Are the motorways in Austria toll-paying?

Yes, you need the "Vignette": it costs 9.80 euros and is valid for 10 days across the entire network. By exiting at Tarvisio you avoid the toll and reach the club via the state road.

Is the club always open?

Yes, all year round, except for 24 December.

What languages are spoken at the reception?

All staff speak Italian, English and German.

Can I use my phone in the club?

No, the use of mobile phones is not allowed. They must be left in the hotel room, in the lockers or in the safes.

How can I contact the girls?

The girls are guests of the club and self-employed workers. Arrangements are made inside the club; the minimum stay is half an hour.

Are electronic payments possible?

Electronic payments are only possible for the bar and hotel services. Self-employed workers can only be paid in cash.

Are there medical checks for the workers in Austria?

Medical checks are mandatory and are carried out every six weeks, with a full examination in particular for sexually transmitted diseases.
